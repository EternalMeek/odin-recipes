Stuffed Dates

Description

Sweet dates stuffed with the tang of Gorgonzola cheese and a walnut piece for some crunch. Mmm!

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 3 ounces crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
  • 36 pitted dates
  • 36 walnut pieces

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese and Gorgonzola cheese together in a bowl.
  2. Cut a slit into each date so they can be spread flat. Spread cheese mixture into each date; top with a walnut piece.
